Who is this for?

Sync is for couples. Particularly those who are in long distance relationships and need an easy way to share their busy lives with each other.

Is my data safe?

Unlike social networks, we respect your privacy and right to own your pictures and conversations. We claim no rights to any of them.

What if I need more features?

We are working on it! Meanwhile, tell us what you would like.

When's the iPhone app coming?

Soon. Meanhile, we would love to have your feedback on our web app!

We are Sync

We are a group of Stanford students dedicated to the mission of building healthier relationships. The fast-paced world around us and proliferating social networks often make us neglect the one most important relationship in our lives - our partner. The eye-witness to your life needs help keeping in sync with your world, especially when you are traveling or living apart.

With this noble purpose, we are building Sync. This is our alpha version and we hope to use our research to bring better, more useful apps to you that will make apositive difference in your life and that of your loved one.
